Responsive Display

There was a time when websites could be made to fit a normal computer screen, and that was that. Now, your customers could be looking at your site on anything from a 5 inch smartphone to a 50 inch curved monitor. It only makes sense to make a website with every screen in mind.

But times are changing even more than that. Gone are the days of having two different sites, one for mobile and one for desktop. Google has stated that they prefer the simpler approach, which is to have a website that dynamically adjusts its display to whatever device its being viewed on.

Of course this doesn't just happen by magic. But I can take care of that.
